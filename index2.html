Dovely is India's Only Bike Taxi Service for Women, Driven by Women. We aim to increase women employment rate in India by hiring women as our Dovely partners and giving them an opportunity to be financially independent and feel empowered. We at Dovely provide safe, easy and cost efficient rides for women.
